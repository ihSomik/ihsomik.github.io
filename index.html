Projects

UniCycle - Campus Bike Sharing

"UniCycle: Campus Bike Sharing" is an innovative project designed to transform university campuses into sustainable and eco-friendly environments. The project introduces a user-friendly mobile app and a web-based dashboard to encourage the use of bicycles, reduce carbon emissions, and foster a sense of community among students, faculty, and staff. The goal is to create a greener and healthier campus by providing an affordable and accessible transportation solution.

Hall's Dining Token Purchase System

The Hall's Dining Token Purchase System is a web-based application designed to streamline the process of purchasing dining tokens for hall meals. It offers a convenient platform for students or residents of a hall to purchase tokens online, reducing the need for manual transactions and paperwork.
